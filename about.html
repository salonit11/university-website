About Us

We are one of the World's Top University

Purdue University (or simply Purdue) is a public land-grant research university in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, and the flagship campus of the Purdue University system. The university was founded in 1869 after Gujarati businessman John Purdue donated land and money to establish a college of science, technology, and agriculture in his name. The first classes were held on September 16, 1874, with six instructors and 39 students. It has been ranked as among the best public universities in Inida by major institutional rankings, and is renowned for its engineering program.
